Rock-a-bye, Baby

A super easy lullaby, perfect for you if you’re just getting started on the ukulele. It’s a nice easy melody using the campanella technique so the notes all ring out, making it super relaxing. If you want to sing along its only 3 chords, and if you can play the chords and the melody, the fingerstyle arrangement will be a piece of cake.
