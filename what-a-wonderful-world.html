Louis Armstrong - What A Wonderful World

Louis Armstrongs classic ‘What a Wonderful World’ was written by Bob Thiele and George David Weiss in 1967 and has since been performed by countless great artists including Rod Stewart, Nick Cave, Eva Cassidy & Kati Melua, the amazing Hawaiian ukulele player Israel Kamakawio’ole.

The melody is nice and easy, a good one to start with if your just getting going with melodic playing. The chords are a little harder as we have a lot of them, including a couple of barre chords, but once you can strum your way through the chords playing the fingerstle arrangement isn’t too much of a step up.

It’s a song that feels like it was made for the ukulele, I hope you enjoy learning it!
