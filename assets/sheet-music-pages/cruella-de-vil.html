Cruella De Vil

This blues inspired Disney classic ‘Cruella De Vil’ from 101 Dalmatians has a little more to dig into that the average Disney song. Plenty of chromatic leading tones and great use of dominant chords. These ideas are good for use in your improvising, think about how they relate to the chords to use them effectively.
