Blue Bossa

Blue Bossa is one of my favourite bossa nova songs, it's not too long so fairly easy to learn but packed with some great musical ideas. It's a got some nice chord extensions and a key change. It's a great example of the Brazilian bossa nova style!

The melody is super easy to learn, but we do have a key change so you’ll be playing 2 different scales. The chords are a little more intricate as we have lots of extensions, but only one bare chord! If you can play both without too much trouble I highly recommend trying the fingerstyle arrangement, it’ll really push your playing to the next level!
