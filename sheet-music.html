Cancellation Policy

If you can’t make your lesson let me know 24 hours in advance so we can rearrange another time that works for both of us. You will be charged the full lesson price for missing a lesson with less than 24 hours notice.

If I can’t attend a Lesson

If I have to cancel a lesson, I will endeavour to give you as much notice as possible and we can rearrange another time that works for both of us.
