Misty

In this arrangement of Erroll Garner’s jazz standard ‘Misty’ I have tried to include a gentle increase of complexity. Starting with the melody, played in a campanella style, then introducing the chords and some arpeggios and then adding in some percussive rhythm. In the music download I have included 3 versions, one with the melody notated in TAB with chord diagrams above so you play it as a duo. A second, fingerstyle version and then the version in the video that gets harder as it goes along.
